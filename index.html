Upload Resources.

With ClassVault, you have the power to effortlessly upload and organize all your class resources in one secure place. Whether it's lecture notes, assignments, or reference materials, our user-friendly platform makes it simple for teachers to share, and students to access, the essential tools they need to succeed.

Securely Store Resources.

At ClassVault, security is our priority. We understand the importance of safeguarding your valuable class resources. Our platform offers state-of-the-art encryption and robust access controls, ensuring that your documents and materials are stored securely. Rest easy knowing that your educational assets are protected, so you can focus on what truly matters - delivering an exceptional learning experience.

Share Resources with Public Link.

With ClassVault, sharing class resources has never been easier. Our intuitive platform allows you to effortlessly generate public links for your documents. Share these links with students, colleagues, or the wider educational community, granting them convenient access to your valuable resources. It's a seamless way to foster collaboration and knowledge sharing, all with just a simple click.

Collaborative Editing.*

ClassVault goes beyond simple storage and sharing. With our collaborative editing feature, teachers and students can work together in real-time on shared documents. Whether it's co-authoring an assignment, fine-tuning a presentation, or leaving comments for feedback, our platform streamlines teamwork and makes collaboration a breeze.
